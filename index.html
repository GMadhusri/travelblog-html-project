Beautiful places i visited...

1. Himachal Pradesh

About :

Himachal Pradesh is a northern Indian state in the Himalayas. It's home to scenic mountain towns and resorts such as Dalhousie. Host to the Dalai Lama, Himachal Pradesh has a strong Tibetan presence. This is reflected in its Buddhist temples and monasteries, as well as its vibrant Tibetan New Year celebrations.

Places to visit in H.P...

  1. Kinnaur Kailash

    It is part of the Himalayas and is close to the Indo-Tibetan border. Manu Adventures India According to Hindu mythology, Lord Shiva and goddess Parvati live in Kinnaur Kailash. Folklore also says that the mountain is the meeting place for the two deities, and that Lord Shiva would host meetings of gods and goddesses there every winter. The Kinnaur Kailash range offers views of other peaks, including Raldang (5,499 meters) and Jorkanden (6,473 meters). To get to Kinnaur Kailash from Delhi, you can take a train to Kalka, then board the narrow-gauge toy train to Shimla. From Shimla, you can take a bus or hire a taxi to reach Kinnaur Kailash.   2. Dharmashala

    Dharamshala is a city in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Surrounded by cedar forests on the edge of the Himalayas, this hillside city is home to the Dalai Lama and the Tibetan government-in-exile. The Thekchen Chöling Temple Complex is a spiritual center for Tibetan Buddhism, while the Library of Tibetan Works and Archives houses thousands of precious manuscripts

2.Ooty

About :
Ooty (short for Udhagamandalam) is a resort town in the Western Ghats mountains, in southern India's Tamil Nadu state. Founded as a British Raj summer resort, it retains a working steam railway line. Other reminders of its colonial past include Stone House, a 19th-century residence, and the circa-1829 St. Stephen’s Church. Its 55-acre Government Botanical Garden lies on the slopes of Doddabetta Peak.
Places to visit...

  1. Pykara Waterfalls

    Pykara is a river 19 kilometres from Ooty in the Indian State of Tamil Nadu with a village by the same name nearby. The Pykara River is considered very sacred by the Todas.

  2. Doddabetta Peak

    3. Doddabetta Tamil pronunciation is the highest mountain in the Nilgiri Mountains at 2,637 metres (8,652 ft). There is a reserved forest area around the peak. It is 9 km from Ooty, on the Ooty-Kotagiri Road in the Nilgiris District of Tamil Nadu
